Reliance is a strong long-term hold with a tactical swing entry opening this week. The biggest swing risk is Brent — every $5/bbl above $90 trims standalone EBITDA by ~₹1,200 Cr/qtr.

  • 2. New-energy capex (solar gigafactory, hydrogen) earnings-positive FY26
  • 3. Retail demerger / listing unlocks SOTP — implied ~₹420/share value

  • 5. Heavy ₹3.1 L Cr debt · refinance cost up 80 bps in 6 months
